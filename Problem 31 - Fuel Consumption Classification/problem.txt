Fuel Consumption Classification 

Scenario: A car’s fuel consumption (km per liter) determines its efficiency rating. 

Input: A decimal value kmPerLiter. 
Output: A string: "Excellent", "Good", or "Poor". 
Rules 
• ≥ 20 → Excellent 
• 10–19 → Good 
• < 10 → Poor 

Sample Input 
Enter car’s fuel consumption (kmPerLitter): 18

Sample Output 
Good 

Constraints: kmPerLiter > 0
