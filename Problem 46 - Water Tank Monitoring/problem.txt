Water Tank Monitoring 

Scenario: A tank has a capacity of 1000 liters. The program repeatedly reads water added per operation. Stop if the tank 
becomes full or overflows. Print "Full" if it reaches exactly 1000, "Overflow" if more. 

Input: Series of integers representing added water. 
Output: Full or Overflow 
Sample Input 
Enter water capacity (L): 
600 
300 
100

Sample Output 
Full
