Count Multiples of 3 and 5 

Scenario: Read n positive integers and count how many of them are multiples of 3, multiples of 5, 
and multiples of both 3 and 5. 

Input: 
First line: integer n (1 ≤ n ≤ 1000). 
Next n lines: one integer each. 
Output: Three integers separated by spaces: <count_mult3> <count_mult5> <count_both>. 

Sample Input 
Enter integers: 
6 
3 
5 
15 
2 
30 
9

Sample Output 
4 3 2
