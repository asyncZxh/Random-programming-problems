EVEN OR ODD COUNTER

Scenario: Count how many numbers in a sequence are even and how many are odd. The sequence ends when the user 
inputs 0 (0 is not counted). 

Input: A series of integers, one per line, ending with 0. 
Output: Two integers separated by a comma: Even: <even_count>, Odd: <odd_count>. 

Sample Input 
Enter a number(s):  
5 
8 
3 
2 
0 

Sample Output 
Even: 2, Odd: 2
