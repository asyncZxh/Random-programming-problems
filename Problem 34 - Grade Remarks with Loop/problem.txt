Grade Remarks with Loop 

Scenario: Ask for grades 5 times and print "Pass" or "Fail" for each. 

Input: Five integer grades (0–100). 
Output: Five lines of "Pass" or "Fail". 
Rule 
• Pass if ≥ 75 
• Otherwise Fail 

Sample Input 
Enter 5 grades: 
80
72 
75 
90 
60

Sample Output 
Pass 
Fail 
Pass 
Pass 
Fail
