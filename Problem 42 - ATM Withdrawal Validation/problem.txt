ATM Withdrawal Validation 

Scenario: An ATM allows withdrawal only if: 
• The amount is a multiple of 100 
• The amount is ≤ current balance 
Print "Approved" or "Denied". 

Input: balance 
amount 
Output: Approved or Denied 

Sample Input 
Enter balance: 5000 
Enter withdrawal amount: 1200 

Sample Output 
Approved
