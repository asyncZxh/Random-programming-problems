Count Numbers Divisible by Both 2 and 3 

Scenario: Given a limit n, count how many numbers from 1 to n are divisible by both 2 and 3 (i.e., divisible by 6).

Input: An integer n. 
Output: Number of integers between 1 and n divisible by both 2 and 3. 

Sample Input 
Enter an integer: 12 

Sample Output 
2
